We're here to help tell your story.

It all starts with your idea. Then through recording, editing and mixing sounds we'll help you create magic.

Snapsound is a boutique post-production sound services company led by Primetime Emmy award-winning sound designer, Zach Seivers.

Our mission is to create dynamic, unique soundtracks for stories across all mediums.

Our Facilities

Our creativity knows no bounds.

Through a collaboration with Deluxe, the
legendary media company with a
worldwide footprint of over 80 mixing
stages and audio suites, we can scale our
talent to meet the needs of creating your
soundtrack.

Whether it's recording ADR in Toronto or
mixing in Dolby Atmos on a state-of-the-
art soundstage in Hollywood, we'll provide
you with an intuitively creative experience
focused on telling your story.
